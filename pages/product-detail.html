How It Works

Magnesium L-Threonate (MgT) was developed by neuroscientists at MIT specifically to deliver magnesium to the brain. Unlike other forms of magnesium, L-Threonate crosses the blood-brain barrier efficiently, increasing brain magnesium levels where they matter most.

Research shows that increasing brain magnesium concentration enhances synaptic density and plasticity in the hippocampus, the brain region critical for learning and memory. Clinical studies have demonstrated improvements in cognitive function, particularly in older adults.
